1. Open Outlook
2. Click "File" in the top left
3. Click "Account Settings"
4. Click "Account Settings" in the dropdown
5. Click the "Change" button
6. Click the "More Settings" button
7. On the "Advanced" tab, click "Add"
8. Enter "%[username]"
- If %[username] does not work, try %[fullname]
9. Click Ok, Ok, Next, Finish (etc to close out all Settings windows)
10. You will see the user's mailbox in the pane on the left.
